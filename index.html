Grocery Monkey

iOS

Smart grocery shopping with barcode scanning and price tracking across stores.

›

Features

  • Barcode scanning with automatic product lookup
  • Track prices across multiple stores
  • Price history charts (6 months, 1 year, all time)
  • Compare regular vs sale prices
  • Store lock feature for quick price entry
  • Premium features for detailed analytics

FAQs

Where does product information come from?
Product data (names, images, brands) comes from Open Food Facts, a free and open database of food products.

Does the app work offline?
Price tracking and lists work offline. Barcode scanning requires internet to look up product information.

What's the difference between free and premium?
Free includes shopping lists and barcode scanning. Premium unlocks full price history, detailed charts, and store comparisons.
