What is Somatic Yoga?

Soma - our living body
Yoga - the union of body, mind and spirit

Somatic yoga is a gentle, mindful movement practice coordinated with breath that connects the body with the brain through the nervous system.

The healing practice of somatic yoga rewires the brain, informing muscles to release and relax and to function as they should.

Somatic yoga uses a technique called Pandiculation to release muscle tensions. Pandiculation focuses on the contraction of muscles, and a slow controlled release and relaxation. Pandiculation releases muscular tension that may have been caused by trauma.

Through awareness we learn about our body, what it needs and doesn't need, and what doesn't serve us.
