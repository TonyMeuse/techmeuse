Frequently Asked Questions

Which measurement scales are supported in Log Calculator?

Log Calculator supports New Brunswick, Ontario, and Doyle scales for board feet calculations, plus firewood volume measurements.

Can I use the apps offline?

Yes, both apps work completely offline with no internet connection required.

How do I request a new feature?

Send an email to techmeuse@gmail.com with your suggestion.

Do the apps collect my data?

No. The apps do not collect personal information, track usage, or share data with third parties.
