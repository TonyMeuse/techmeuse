Smart Grocery Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Smart Grocery Tracker is developed by Anthony Meuse. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Smart Grocery Tracker handles your information.

Data Collection

Smart Grocery Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter into the app — including product names, prices, and store information — is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Smart Grocery Tracker uses the Open Food Facts API (https://world.openfoodfacts.org/) to retrieve product information when scanning barcodes. No personal data is sent — only the barcode number is used to look up product details. The app also uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for the optional Premium upgrade, which is handled entirely by Apple under their own privacy policy.

Data Storage

All app data is saved locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed. We have no access to your data at any time.

Children's Privacy

Smart Grocery Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our support page at tonymeuse.github.io/techmeuse.
